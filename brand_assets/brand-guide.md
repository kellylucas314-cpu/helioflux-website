# HelioFlux Brand Guide

## Identity
**Company:** HelioFlux
**Tagline:** Your cells emit light. We learned to read it.
**Category:** Biophotonic cancer detection

## Voice
"The Ambitious Realist" — mechanism over metaphor, conservative precision, warm but not soft.
Not a wellness brand. Not a pharma brand. A science company with urgency.

## Colors
| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Navy (primary dark) | #0f1c2e | Hero, CTA, footer, dark breaks |
| Navy (secondary dark) | #16263A | Text on light, some sections |
| Warm Cream | #f5f0eb | Body/light section default |
| Off-White | #F7F6F2 | Alternate light sections |
| Cyan (accent) | #00A9D6 | Accent ONLY — sparingly |
| Cyan (glow) | #00c9c8 | Logo teal, emission effects |
| Soft White | rgba(255,255,255,0.88) | Text on dark backgrounds |
| Muted White | rgba(255,255,255,0.55) | Secondary text on dark |

## Typography
- **Headings:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Body:** DM Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Heading weight:** 300-400 (light to regular — never bold/heavy)
- **Body size:** 18-20px with line-height 1.7
- **Eyebrows:** uppercase, letter-spacing 0.15em, 11-12px, muted opacity

## Logo
- Current: SVG text wordmark — "HelioFlux" in Plus Jakarta Sans
- Color on dark: white (#ffffff) with teal (#00c9c8) accent
- Color on light: navy (#0f1c2e)
- Concepts in `logo-concepts/` — Φ (phi) mon direction, not yet implemented

## Photography
- Hero: woman in dramatic light, dark/cinematic treatment
- Overlay: dark + blue tint so white text reads clearly
- Style: warm, human, not clinical/cold

## DO NOT
- Em dashes anywhere
- Gradients
- Drop shadows (heavy)
- Orange — this is not a medtech brand from 2021
- Serif fonts for body or headings (Glenn hates serifs)
- Animation that feels bouncy or aggressive
- Pure black (#000) — use navy instead
- Pure white (#fff) for text on dark — use rgba(255,255,255,0.88)
