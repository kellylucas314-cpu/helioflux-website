The Problem

HelioFlux detects at 5–10 million cells. Something you cannot feel yet. Something that hasn't spread. Something that's still easy to treat.

The Science

Every living cell
emits light.

Ultra-weak photon emissions (UPEs) are a real-time signal of cellular metabolism — thousands of times fainter than a firefly. When metabolism changes, the light changes. We built the detector to hear the difference.

Our device doesn't shine light into you. It records the light already coming out.

Photomultiplier tubes detect single photons — the same sensitivity used in particle physics, now applied to human biology.

01

Non-Invasive

Place the affected area near the detector. No blood draw. No biopsy. No contrast agent. No preparation required.

02

Single-Photon Sensitive

Our photomultiplier tubes detect light at the single-photon level — the same instrumentation used to study the cosmos, applied to cellular biology.

03

Pattern Analysis

Wavelength-specific signatures distinguish healthy from malignant tissue with 90–92% discriminant accuracy across 13 cell lines.
