How It Works

Two systems. One answer.

HelioFlux combines proprietary hardware and AI to capture and classify the faint light your cells already emit.

Hardware

QSense™

Captures ultra-weak photon emissions at single-photon sensitivity. Non-invasive. No radiation.

Software

LuminAI™

AI classifies photon patterns against a validated cancer signature database. Real-time results.
